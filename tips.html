💡 Green Tips

  • 🚴 Use a bicycle or public transport for short distances.
  • 💧 Fix leaking faucets to save hundreds of liters of water.
  • 🔌 Unplug chargers when not in use to save energy.
  • 🌱 Grow indoor plants to naturally purify air.
  • 🛍️ Carry a reusable bag instead of using plastic bags.
